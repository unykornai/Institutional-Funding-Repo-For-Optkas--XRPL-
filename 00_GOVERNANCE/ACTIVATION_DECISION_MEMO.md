## DEACTIVATION

Any activated track may be deactivated by the Manager at any time. Upon deactivation:

1. All outstanding positions must be closed or transferred
2. All counterparties must be notified within 5 business days
3. Final reporting delivered
4. Wallets drained to zero and monitoring continues for 90 days
5. Deactivation recorded in this memo's amendment section
